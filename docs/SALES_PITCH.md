## Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)

**ARKSpace** is a complete coworking and shared-space management platform built natively on the Frappe/ERPNext ecosystem. It gives operators everything they need to run their spaces — bookings, memberships, contracts, training, CRM, billing — in a single, integrated system. No API glue, no third-party subscriptions, no data silos.

## Problem Statement

Coworking operators face a fragmented software landscape:

| Pain Point | Typical Workaround | Cost |
|------------|-------------------|------|
| Space booking | Calendly + Google Calendar | $15–30/mo |
| Membership management | Spreadsheets or Nexudus | $100–400/mo |
| Billing & invoicing | Separate accounting software | $30–80/mo |
| CRM & lead tracking | HubSpot or manual follow-up | $0–50/mo |
| Contracts & legal docs | Word templates, manual signing | Time cost |
| Member portal | Custom development | $5,000+ one-time |
| Reporting & analytics | Manual Excel exports | Hours/week |

**Total cost of fragmentation:** $200–600/month + hours of manual reconciliation.

---

## Solution: ARKSpace

One app. One database. Zero fragmentation.

### Core Value Propositions

1. **Native ERPNext Integration**  
   Bookings auto-create invoices. Memberships link to customers. No sync, no middleware.

2. **Complete Lifecycle Management**  
   From lead → tour → membership → booking → billing → renewal — fully automated.

3. **Bilingual by Design**  
   Full Arabic + English support. RTL-ready. Dual-language contracts. 700+ translated strings.

4. **Open Source & Self-Hosted**  
   MIT license. Own your data. No per-seat pricing. No vendor lock-in.

5. **Built for Operators**  
   Interactive floor plans, real-time occupancy, bulk operations, member portals.

## Feature Highlights

### 🏢 Space Management
- Visual floor plans with color-coded availability
- 6 space types: Hot Desk, Dedicated Desk, Private Office, Meeting Room, Event Space, Virtual Office
- Real-time occupancy tracking with live updates
- Amenity management with flexible pricing

### 📅 Booking Engine
- Hourly, daily, and monthly bookings
- Auto check-in/check-out with no-show detection
- Bulk operations for front-desk efficiency
- Conflict detection and availability checking

### 🎫 Membership Platform
- 6 plan types with tiered pricing (monthly/quarterly/yearly)
- Credit wallet system for prepaid hours
- Auto-renewal and expiry notifications
- Member dashboard with unified view

### 📊 CRM Pipeline
- Lead tracking from 6 acquisition channels
- Tour scheduling with outcome tracking
- One-click conversion to customer + membership
- Full pipeline: New → Contacted → Tour → Negotiating → Converted

### 📝 Contracts & Legal
- Jinja-powered contract templates (Arabic, English, Bilingual)
- Digital document management (10 document types)
- Payment receipt generation with 6 payment methods
- 3 print formats: Booking Confirmation, Membership Card, Receipt

### 🎓 Training & Community
- Learning management with modules and sessions
- Gamification with badges (Bronze → Platinum)
- Progress tracking per member
- Community building through shared learning

### 💰 Billing Integration
- Auto-creates Sales Invoices on booking/membership submit
- Handles cancellation credits
- Employee-customer linking for HRMS users
- Unpaid invoice tracking

### 📈 Analytics
- Revenue Summary report
- Space Occupancy report
- Membership Analytics report
- Dashboard with 4 number cards + 5 charts

## Ideal Customer Profile

### Primary: Coworking Space Operators
- **Size:** 1–50 locations
- **Region:** Middle East, North Africa, and global
- **Stack:** Already using or considering ERPNext
- **Pain:** Juggling 3+ tools for space management
- **Budget:** Prefer CapEx (one-time) over OpEx (monthly SaaS)

### Secondary: Corporate Flex-Space Managers
- Companies with internal shared desks, meeting rooms, training spaces
- Need integration with existing ERP for billing and HR
- Require audit trail and compliance features

### Tertiary: Training Centers & Community Hubs
- Organizations that combine coworking with education/training
- Need member management + learning management in one system
