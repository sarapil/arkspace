# ARKSpace Help

Welcome to ARKSpace — your complete coworking space management platform.

## Getting Started

After logging in, you'll find the ARKSpace workspace on your desk with:
- **Dashboard** showing bookings, memberships, and revenue stats
- **Quick actions** for common tasks
- **Navigation** to all modules

## Modules

- **Spaces** — Manage space types, amenities, and individual spaces
- **Bookings** — Create and manage space bookings with check-in/out
- **Memberships** — Subscription plans with credit wallets
- **CRM** — Lead pipeline and tour scheduling
- **Contracts** — Legal documents and contract management
- **Training** — Learning modules with badges and progress tracking

## Key Pages

- **Floor Plan** (`/desk/arkspace/floor-plan`) — Interactive visual floor plan
- **ARK Live** (`/desk/arkspace/ark-live`) — Real-time space monitoring
